# YT Study Timer

A Chrome Extension that detects educational videos on YouTube and helps you track your study time.

## Features
- **AI-Powered Detection**: Uses Gemini AI to analyze video content and determine if it's educational.
- **Smart Notifications**: Notifies you when an educational video is playing on YouTube.
- **Session Timer**: Start, pause, and reset a timer to track your study sessions.
- **Session History**: View and manage your past study sessions.
- **Video Redirection**: Click to jump directly to the video you were watching.

## Installation

1.  Clone or download the repository.
2.  Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
3.  Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in the top-right corner).
4.  Click the "Load unpacked" button.
5.  Select the folder containing the extension files.

## Usage

1.  Navigate to a YouTube video.
2.  If the video is detected as educational, you will see a notification.
3.  Open the extension popup to view your study session timer.
4.  Click "START" to begin tracking your study time.
5.  Click "PAUSE" to temporarily stop the timer.
6.  Click "SAVE" to record the session and add it to your history.
